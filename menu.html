Vegan Delights

The vegan delights package is all about avoiding meats or any other animal product. We understand customers concerns about the use of animal products along with dietary problems with others.

Papaya Salad

If you've never tried papaya salad then you better try it at Package Delights! Papaya salad is created with shredded pieces of papaya and other vegtables. Although it seems to be quite refreshing do not be fooled it has a rather strong kick to it. The spice level will vary from 1-3 and can include either crab or shrimp.

Pad Thai

Pad Thai is the most recognized dish in Thai culture, but who could blame them. The stir fried noodle dish is covered in a delicious sauce with shrimp or chicken, while garnishes are also included increase the amount of flavor Pad thai is a combination of sweet, spicy, and tart all in one bite.

Nasi Lemak

This malaysian dish is a national favorite, as it is packed with immense flavor. Unlike other places, we cook our nasi lemak with pandan leaves, which absorbs much more flavor. The dish includes cocunut infused rice, a dollop of spicy sambal, a hard boiled egg, and slices of cucumber and crunchy fried peanuts.

Vegetarian Delights

Vegetarian delights are created to also offer an option that follows a general vegetarian diet, limiting any and all processed foods but also focusing on healthy fats that provide omega-3 fatty acids.

Lasagna

This layered goodness is packed with meat, sauce, and the perfect amount of cheese. With each bite, you'll taste the perfect combination of pasta and it's other ingredients which enhance the flavor.

Pizza

Who doesn't love the traditional pizza? We sure don't. Customize your pizza with our various toppings and bases or simply pick one from our menu. We offer the traditional cheese and pepperoni pizza with your side of sauces.

Wings

Our wings can either be served to you the traditional way or the boneless way. Like the usual order, our wings will be served with traditional celery and a side of fries. Be sure to also cusotmize your wings with our sauces of the day!

Gluten-free Delights

Like with the vegan and vegetarian options above, we provide a gluten-free option for those who do not want to eat gluten food products.

Bucatini

This delicious dish includes pasta and a specially made sauce that will have your taste buds excited. The bowl will be served containing a few leafy greens and cheese that will be customized to your personal liking.

Kebabs

If you haven't tried kebabs, well you better try them here. The meat is saturated with our special seasoning and also have other things on it that you would desire like a fruit here and there to break the flavor.

Ratatouille

This is a vegetable dish consisting of onions, zucchini, tomatoes, eggplant, and peppers, fried and stewed in oil. The delicious dish is usually arranged in a circular pattern making it more appealing than it already is.

Protien-based Delights

We also carry a heavily protein-based package offering a variety of protein-packed foods like meats, eggs, various types of nuts, and any other normal food you could think of. This pack aims to provide a way to obtain a heartier meal plan for those who may workout, or in general need more protein consumption.

Cheeseburger

Cheeseburgers is the one meal no one can turn away. We offer your burger in different ways. For example, you can have it with extra cheese, no tomatoes, or be adventurous and have it with different filling like bacon.

Chicken & Waffles

Waffles are outstanding and chicken is just amazing -- so why not combine both and have the perfect combination. Our waffles are served crispy on the outside but also fluffy on the inside. The chicken is also fried to golden perfection.

Jambalya

This culture fusioned dish is rice mixed with a combination of vegtables, shrimp, and chicken. The rice is seasoned and flavored to create something spicy, savory, and a tinge bit sweet as well. Try out the dish for some Louisiana flavor.
